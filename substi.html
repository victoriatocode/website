Substance + STI

Women's Health

I designed my NIH/NIDA F31-funded dissertation, Barriers to Prenatal Care in the Context of Maternal Methamphetamine Use and Congenital Syphilis in Los Angeles County, 2011–2020, to ask where methamphetamine use, stigma, and pregnancy collide in the syphilis treatment cascade. I integrated multilevel, spatial, and longitudinal epidemiologic analyses to follow how structural factors shape who is screened, who is treated, and whose care falls apart.
