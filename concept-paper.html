Research Brief

Double Jeopardy Study

This concept paper, prepared in March 2022 as the UC Speaks Up Asian study began, lays out the initial rationale, aims, methods, timeline, and team for our 12-month mixed-methods investigation of the sexual violence, sexual harassment, and anti-Asian xenophobia that University of California students from Asian communities have experienced during the COVID-19 pandemic. The study plan evolved over the course of the project, and the finalized design is documented in the study protocol.
